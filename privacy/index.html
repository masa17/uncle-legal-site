1. Zero data collection

We don’t collect any kind of data, including but not limited to:

  • Personal information
  • Device information
  • Location data
  • Usage data
  • Server logs

2. Designed to protect your privacy

Uncle works offline:

  • All data is stored on your device.
  • All operations are processed and executed locally.
  • No personal information is sold, shared, or used for marketing purposes.

3. Subscriptions

Uncle offers optional subscriptions (monthly or annual) with a free 3-day trial. Payment is processed through Apple’s App Store. We do not receive or store your payment information.

4. Data Storage

All app usage data remains on your device. We do not store or transmit any personal content or identifiable data to external servers.

5. Age Suitability

Uncle is designed for users 9 years and older. All controls are voluntary and under the user’s control. The app does not track or report on personal activity to third parties.

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Modify app settings or usage limits at any time
  • Delete the app and all local data
  • Cancel any subscription via your Apple ID account settings

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted within the app and on the App Store listing.
